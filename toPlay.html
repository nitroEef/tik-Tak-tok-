How To Play

This game is created for two players. the first player will carry "O" tiles, while the second player will carry the "X" tiles respectively. The goal of tic-tac-toe is to be the first player to get three in a row on a 3-by-3 grid. To start, one player draws a board, creating a grid of squares, In a 3-by-3 grid game, the player who is playing "O" will go first. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the board until either player has three in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally or until all squares on the grid are filled. If a player is able to draw three Xs or three Os in a row, then that player wins. If all squares are filled and neither player has made a complete row of Xs or Os, then the game is a draw.
